Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 March 2026

Munajat: Dua Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address (used for authentication via Supabase).
  • Du'ā input text: The thoughts you type or speak are sent to our AI service to generate personalised prayers. These inputs are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after generation.
  • Saved du'ās: Prayers you choose to save are stored securely in your account.
  • Journal entries: Any journal entries you create are stored securely in your account.
  • Location data: With your permission, approximate location is used solely to determine prayer times via the AlAdhan API. Location data is not stored.
  • Subscription status: Managed by Apple (App Store) and RevenueCat. We do not process or store payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and provide access to the app.
  • To generate personalised du'ās based on your input.
  • To display accurate prayer times for your location.
  • To manage your subscription status.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and secure data storage.
  • OpenAI — Du'ā generation (your input text is sent to OpenAI's API; see OpenAI's privacy policy).
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management.
  • AlAdhan API — Prayer time calculations.
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing.

4. Data Retention

Your saved du'ās and journal entries are retained as long as your account is active. Du'ā input text used for generation is processed in real time and not retained. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication through Supabase. Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Export your saved du'ās and journal entries.
  • Delete your account entirely.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by email.
