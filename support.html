Frequently Asked Questions

How does Munajat generate du'ās?

Munajat uses advanced AI guided by strict Islamic principles. Every du'ā is structured following authentic Quran and Sunnah methodology, incorporating the Names of Allah and verified hadith sources.

Are the hadiths authentic?

Yes. We only use Sahih (authentic) and Hasan (good) hadiths from recognised scholars including Ibn al-Qayyim, Ibn Taymiyyah, and Ibn Rajab. No weak or fabricated hadiths are used.

How do I manage my subscription?

Your subscription is managed through the Apple App Store. To change or cancel: open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Munajat: Dua Studio.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at munajatduastudio@gmail.com and we will delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use encrypted connections and secure cloud infrastructure. Your du'ā input is processed in real time and not stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
