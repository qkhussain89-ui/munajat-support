Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Munajat: Dua Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Your first name, last name, and email address (used for authentication and personalisation).
  • Du'ā input text: The thoughts you type or speak are sent to our AI service to generate personalised supplications. See Section 2 below for full details.
  • Saved du'ās: Du'ās you choose to save are stored securely in your account.
  • Journal entries: Any journal entries you create are stored securely in your account.
  • Location data: With your permission, approximate location is used solely to determine prayer times via the AlAdhan API. Location data is not stored.
  • Subscription status: Managed by Apple (App Store) and RevenueCat. We do not process or store payment information.

2. AI-Powered Du'ā Generation

Important: Munajat uses artificial intelligence to generate personalised du'ās based on the thoughts, words, and intentions you share. Before your first du'ā is generated, the app asks for your explicit consent.

What data is sent

When you create a du'ā, the following is sent to our AI partner for processing:

  • The text you type or speak as your du'ā input
  • Your selected language preference
  • Your du'ā settings (length, focus area)

No personal account information (your name, email, or password) is ever sent to our AI partner.

Who processes this data

Your du'ā input is processed by OpenAI (openai.com), which generates your personalised du'ā based on Qur'ānic and Sunnah principles. OpenAI's own privacy and data usage policies apply to this processing.

How this data is used

Your input is used solely to generate your du'ā. It is not used for advertising, marketing, model training, or any other purpose.

Your consent

Before your first du'ā is generated, the app presents a clear consent dialog explaining this data sharing. You must explicitly agree before any data is transmitted to OpenAI. If you decline, no data is sent and the du'ā generation feature will not function.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and provide access to the app.
  • To generate personalised du'ās based on your input (via OpenAI).
  • To save and organise your du'ās and journal entries.
  • To display accurate prayer times for your location.
  • To manage your subscription status.

We do not use your information for targeted advertising. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI — Processes your du'ā input text to generate personalised supplications (see Section 2).
  • Supabase — Authentication and secure data storage for your account, saved du'ās, and journal entries.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management.
  • AlAdhan API — Prayer time calculations.
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing (all payments handled securely by Apple).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your saved du'ās and journal entries are retained as long as your account is active. Du'ā input text used for generation is processed in real time by OpenAI and not retained by us after the du'ā is generated.

You may at any time:

  • Delete individual du'ās from within the app.
  • Delete journal entries from within the app.
  • Delete your entire account from the app's Settings (Me → Delete Account), which permanently removes all your data from our servers.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication through Supabase. Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Export your saved du'ās and journal entries.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw your consent for AI data processing at any time.

9. Charitable Commitment

10% of Munajat's revenue is donated to charitable causes across the UK and around the world. This commitment does not involve the sharing of any user data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
